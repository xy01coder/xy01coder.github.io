Quick Solutions

üé§ Recording Issues

Issue: Cannot record voice

Solution: Ensure microphone permission is enabled in Settings → VoiceMinder → Microphone. Make sure no other app is using the microphone.

üîî Alarm Not Working

Issue: Not receiving reminders

Solution: Ensure notification permissions are enabled in Settings → VoiceMinder → Notifications. Also check that Do Not Disturb mode is not active.

System Requirements

  • iOS: 13.0 or later
  • Permissions: Microphone and Notification access required
  • Storage: Minimal storage required for voice recordings (up to 60 seconds per reminder)

Features Guide

Creating a Reminder

  1. Tap the "+" button on the home screen
  2. Record your voice message (up to 60 seconds)
  3. Preview your recording by tapping the play button
  4. Enter a title and optional description
  5. Set the reminder time
  6. Choose repeat option (one-time, daily, weekly)
  7. Save your reminder

Managing Reminders

  • View all reminders on the home screen
  • Swipe left to delete, edit, or disable a reminder
  • Tap on a reminder to edit it
  • Toggle the switch to enable/disable reminders

When Alarm Rings

  • Your recorded voice will play as the alarm
  • Tap the notification or the stop button to dismiss
  • For repeat reminders, the next alarm is automatically scheduled

Troubleshooting Steps

  1. Restart the App: Force quit and relaunch VoiceMinder
  2. Check Permissions: Verify both microphone and notification permissions are granted in Settings → VoiceMinder
  3. Update the App: Ensure you have the latest version installed
  4. Restart Your Device: Sometimes a device restart resolves notification issues
  5. Reinstall: As a last resort, uninstall and reinstall the app (note: this will delete all your reminders)
