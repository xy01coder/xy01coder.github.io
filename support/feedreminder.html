System Requirements

  • iOS: 13.0 or later
  • Permissions: Notification access required
  • Storage: Minimal storage required (app stores up to 100 feeding records)

Features Guide

Recording a Feeding

  1. Tap the "Fed" button on the home screen
  2. The app will record the current time and schedule the next reminder
  3. View your feeding history by tapping the history icon

Managing History

  • View all past feeding records in the History screen
  • Swipe left on any record to delete it
  • The app stores up to 100 records automatically

Customizing Settings

  • Feeding Interval: Adjust the time between feedings (hours and minutes)
  • Alarm Sound: Choose your preferred reminder sound
  • Volume: Set the alarm volume level

Troubleshooting Steps

  1. Restart the App: Force quit and relaunch FeedReminder
  2. Check Permissions: Verify notification permissions are granted
  3. Update the App: Ensure you have the latest version installed
  4. Restart Your Device: Sometimes a device restart resolves notification issues
  5. Reinstall: As a last resort, uninstall and reinstall the app (note: this will clear your history)
