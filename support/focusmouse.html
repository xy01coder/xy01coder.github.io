System Requirements

  • macOS: 11.5 (Big Sur) or later
  • Architecture: Intel x86_64 and Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3) supported
  • Permissions: Accessibility access required
  • Multiple Displays: At least 2 connected displays recommended

Troubleshooting Steps

  1. Restart the App: Quit and relaunch FocusMouse
  2. Check Permissions: Verify accessibility permissions are granted
  3. Reset Settings: Remove preferences file: ~/Library/Preferences/com.yourcompany.FocusMouse.plist
  4. Restart macOS: Sometimes a system restart resolves display detection issues
